About

Designing for complexity
since 2015.

I'm a Product Designer who thrives in environments where the problems are tangled and the stakes are real. My work spans airlines, legal technology, and e-commerce — industries where getting the details wrong costs real money and real trust.

I bring a collaborative, research-driven approach to every project. Whether I'm listening to live customer calls at an airline call center or surveying shop owners in a Facebook group, I go to where the insights are.

When the product ships, I stick around to see what's working and what isn't — because design doesn't end at the handoff.
